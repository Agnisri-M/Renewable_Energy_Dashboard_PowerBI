# Renewable Energy Dashboard 🌱⚡

## Project Overview
An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing renewable energy generation, consumption, and sustainability metrics across 10 Indian states from 2020-2024.

## Tool Used
- Power BI Desktop
- Microsoft Excel / CSV

## Dataset
- Records: 1,00,000 rows
- Columns: 17
- Years: 2020-2024
- Regions: 10 Indian States
- Energy Sources: Solar, Wind, Hydro, Biomass, Solar-Wind Hybrid

## KPI Metrics
| KPI | Value |
|-----|-------|
| Total Energy Generated | 43.23M MWh |
| Total Energy Consumed | 34.15M MWh |
| Power Deficit | 9.08M MWh |
| CO2 Saved | 35.45M Tons |
| Total Records | 100K |

## Dashboard Pages
1. **Executive Overview** - KPI Cards, Slicers, Charts
2. **Generation Dashboard** - Month wise & Region wise trends
3. **Regional Dashboard** - State wise performance
4. **Sustainability Dashboard** - CO2, Deficit, Weather analysis

## Key Insights
- Hydro energy contributes highest generation (30.84%)
- Sunny & Clear weather produces maximum energy
- Tamil Nadu & Punjab lead in regional performance
- Power deficit decreasing trend over months
